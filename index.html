Our Story

Fourteen years of building on trust, not just concrete.

50+
Homes & Buildings Completed

Founded in 2010, Sunshine Construction started with a simple promise: build every project as if it were our own family's home.

Since then we've delivered more than 50 residential and commercial projects across Chennai — from compact family homes to multi-unit apartments — without losing the personal attention a first-time client deserves.

Every site is run by our own supervisors, every material is chosen for the Chennai climate, and every client gets a direct line to the team building their project — not a call centre.
